Meizhi Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We respect your privacy. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and always under your control.

1. Introduction

Meizhi is an AI-powered meal planning app that helps you eat better every day. We generate personalized daily meal plans, send timely reminders, and learn your preferences over time.

2. AI & Automated Generation

Meizhi uses AI (currently Google Gemini) to generate personalized meal plans. We share anonymized dietary preferences (not personal info like name or email) with our AI service provider to generate meal suggestions.

3. Information We Collect

  • Email address — for account creation and authentication
  • Display name — optional, personalizes your experience
  • Dietary preferences — cuisines, restrictions, and allergies you choose
  • Push notification tokens — to deliver meal reminders
  • Meal plan data — AI-generated meals, cooking history, and favorites
  • Device timezone — to schedule meal generation at 1:00 AM local time

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate 12 personalized meals daily tailored to your preferences
  • Send meal reminder notifications before each meal time
  • Maintain your cooking history and streak statistics
  • Improve AI meal generation quality over time
  • Authenticate your account securely across sessions

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in Supabase (SOC 2 Type II certified). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data is permanently removed within 24 hours.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication
  • Apple Sign-In — optional authentication
  • Google Gemini — AI for meal generation
  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Expo Push Notifications — for meal reminders
  • Pexels & TheMealDB — ingredient images

8. Push Notifications

We send meal reminder notifications based on your timing preferences. You can disable notifications at any time from app settings.

9. Cookies

We do not use cookies. Meizhi is a mobile app and does not store any cookies on your device.

10. Health Disclaimer

Meizhi is a meal planning tool and is not a medical service. Meal suggestions are AI-generated and should not replace professional dietary advice.

11. Age Requirement

Meizhi is intended for users aged 13 and older.

12. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Access: View all your data in the app
  • Edit: Update preferences from Profile settings
  • Delete: Use "Delete Account" in Profile to erase all data
  • Export: Contact us at mirkazuki.app@gmail.com

13. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Right to access, rectify, erase, and data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • International data transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses

14. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at mirkazuki.app@gmail.com

By using Meizhi you agree to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time.
